Fast and Furious

Fast and Furious is a series of movies that for a broad explaination is about a group of friends that call themseleves a family. What has brought such different people together is street racing. Throughout the series of movies they face different difficulties and by the end of all eight movies not everyone who they started with survived all of their constant battles with the law and more.

Some actors in this movie include:

  • Paul Walker
  • Vin Diesel
  • Michelle Rodriguez
  • Jordana Brewster
